ForestPA: ForestPA: Constructs a Decision Forest by Penalizing Attributes used in Previous Trees.

URL:http://csusap.csu.edu.au/~zislam/
Author:Michael Furner
Maintainer:Michael Furner <mfurner{[at]}csu.edu.au>
