Description:Resamples a dataset by applying the Synthetic Minority Oversampling TEchnique (SMOTE). The original dataset must fit entirely in memory. The amount of SMOTE and number of nearest neighbors may be specified. For more information, see

Nitesh V. Chawla et. al. (2002). Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique. Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research. 16:321-357.
